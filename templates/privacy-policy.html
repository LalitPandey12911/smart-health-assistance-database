Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Smart Health Assistant platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Symptoms you input for diagnosis purposes.
  • Optional form data (name, email, message) from the contact form.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-driven health predictions and related suggestions.
  • To respond to queries submitted via the contact form.

3. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your information, but please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Access

We do not share your personal data with third parties. All predictions are processed locally within our application.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with a revised date.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please reach out via our Contact Us page.
